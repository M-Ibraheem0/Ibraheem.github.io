Executive Summary

A mid-sized e-commerce marketplace was struggling with fragmented data across 12+ systems, manual reporting processes, and lack of strategic insights. Seller churn was high, customer acquisition costs were rising, and operational inefficiencies were limiting growth potential.

Our comprehensive data transformation delivered a unified analytics platform with real-time insights, predictive models, and automated decision-making systems. The result: $15M in identified GMV growth opportunities, $1.8M in operational savings, and a 35% reduction in seller churn.
